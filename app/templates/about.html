Community

[ kuh-myoo-ni-tee ]

A group of people living in the same locality.
A group of people having the same interests.

Agriculture

[ ag-ri-kuhl-cher ]

The science, art, and business of cultivating soil, producing crops, and raising livestock; farming.


While volunteering in rural Nepal, I saw how agriculture could bring a village together. We're so connected now but we live disconnected from our world and our neighbors. This is a project in rediscovering ourselves while tuning ourselves to our surroundings. I believe that through collective action we can close the gap that haw grown between agriculture and community, these two things so essential to us. Follow me on this journey. Let's start by making the map.

--Mark Ehler
